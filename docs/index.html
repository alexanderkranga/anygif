Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

AnyGif is a Telegram bot that converts any video URL into a GIF. Just paste a link, choose the moment you want, and get your GIF delivered right in the chat.

Each GIF costs 10 Telegram Stars. Stars are Telegram's built-in digital currency that you can purchase directly within the app.

If your GIF fails to generate for any reason, your 10 Stars are automatically refunded. You'll never be charged for a GIF you didn't receive.

AnyGif supports over 1,800 websites including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Reddit, Vimeo, Twitch, adult sites, and many more. If it's a video on the internet, there's a good chance it works.

GIFs can be up to 10 seconds long. You choose the start time and duration when creating your GIF.

Open @anygif_bot in Telegram and send any video URL. The bot will guide you through selecting the start time and duration, then generate your GIF after payment.

We recommend adding AnyGif as an exception so your GIFs aren't automatically saved to your camera roll. On iOS: go to Settings → Data and Storage → Save to Photos → Private Chats → Add Exception, then select AnyGif and set it to save neither photos nor videos. On Android: go to Settings → Data and Storage → Save to Gallery → Add Exception, then select AnyGif and set it to save neither photos nor videos.

Nothing. Your video link and request are used to create your GIF and then immediately discarded. We have no record of what you asked for, who you are, or that you ever used the bot. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

You can purchase Telegram Stars directly within the Telegram app. Go to Settings → My Stars, or simply try to pay for a GIF and Telegram will prompt you to buy Stars if you don't have enough.
