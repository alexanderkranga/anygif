Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

AnyGif Bot (@anygif_bot) is a fully anonymous Telegram bot service. Your privacy isn't just a feature - it's a core design principle. This policy explains our approach to data: we simply don't collect any.

Data We Do NOT Collect

AnyGif does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user IDs or chat IDs are stored
  • No video URLs are collected or logged
  • No generated GIFs or links are stored anywhere
  • No cookies, no analytics, no tracking on this website
  • No personal information of any kind

How the Bot Works

When you send a video URL to AnyGif Bot, the video is processed in real-time to generate your GIF. Once the GIF is delivered to you in Telegram, all temporary processing data is immediately discarded. Nothing persists on our servers - no URLs, no files, no metadata.

Payments

All payments are handled entirely by Telegram's Stars system. AnyGif does not process, access, or store any payment information. Transaction records exist only within Telegram's infrastructure, under their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

AnyGif uses the following third-party services solely for operation:

  • Telegram Bot API - for receiving messages and sending GIFs. Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloud infrastructure - for real-time video processing only. No data is retained after processing completes.

Your Rights

You can block or delete the bot at any time from your Telegram chat list. Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to request, export, or delete. You are completely in control.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Given that we don't collect data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact
