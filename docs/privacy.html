Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

We don't know who you are

AnyGif (@anygif_bot) was built so that we genuinely cannot identify you. There's no account, no sign-up, and no profile. When you use the bot, you are completely anonymous - not just in policy, but by design.

We never see what you send

When you send a video link to the bot, that link is used to create your GIF and then immediately thrown away. We do not log it, store it, or look at it. By the time your GIF arrives, the request is gone. There is no history of what you asked for.

Nothing is stored after your GIF is delivered

Once your GIF is sent to you, nothing remains on our side. There is no database of users, no record of what was requested, and no files kept anywhere. There is literally nothing to leak, because there is nothing to keep.

Payments are handled by Telegram - not us

You pay with Telegram Stars, Telegram's own built-in currency. The entire payment process happens inside Telegram. We never see your payment details, your card information, or anything related to how you pay. If you have questions about payments, Telegram's privacy policy applies.

You can verify everything

Every privacy claim on this page is backed by public source code. AnyGif is fully open source - anyone can read the code and confirm that it does exactly what we say. We're not asking you to trust a policy document. We're inviting you to check the code yourself.
